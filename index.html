Welcome to EcoDistrict Pittsburgh

Uptown Neighborhood Steering Committee

The Uptown EcoDistrict area consist of Uptown community members collaborating with the City of Pittsburgh and regional environmental groups, developers, and other commercial public/private endeavors to use public policy, planning, and civic development to seed and grow a grow the global green neighborhood movement and community lifestyle in the Uptown area of Pittsburgh, PA.

Steering Committee

The 2015 Steering Committee consists of self-elected and invited neighborhood business owners, residents, community organizations, City of Pittsburgh officials, and regional organizations vested in community-wide, long-term infrastructure planning and development.
